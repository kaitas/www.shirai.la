Two weeks ago…

Published by nine on May 29, 2015

 

So this is my first part of my internship at KAIT, Japan. I arrive at HANEDA Airport with my 5 survivors in May 20th. Very tired and jet lag!. The first week is to busy, I have a lot of thing to do and meet new friends (but we call “family”). Japanese daily conversations make me confuse too much. I know Thai culture and Japanese culture are difference, so I hope I can learn from each other and feel more relax in the future.

KAIT

FAMILY

I get a great welcome from the KAIT, which is what I am impressed. Everybody is trying to talk to me, tells their stories and shows me who they are that makes me have a lot of new friends. This is the first time that I leave from my house for a long time. So this is the first time that I have to cook by myself and I never thought that I have really enjoyed doing it. Several kinds of item, including food ingredients is cheaper than Thailand. That made me surprised.

AIST

JAXA

First week, I went to Miraikan on May 24 , it is a place where I can understand the things happening in our world today from a scientific point of view and have discussions while considering the future that awaits us. I gain the new experiences from here and inspired me in many ways. I fall in love with Asimo, I’m really very happy to see the real Asimo not only watch in the television. I had heard about it since I was young in Thailand. I think this technology can change the world. I am grateful to KAIT that gave me the opportunity to take me on a field trip. Furthermore, last Tuesday I went to AIST and JAXA; the place which has the high technology, I really enjoyed to watch the Space technology and buy souvenirs for my family and friends.

NHK science & technology research laboratories

TEPIA

NINE  & SHIRAI Sensei

The second week is still busy and very serious but at the same time, it was a very good week for me. I’ve had the opportunity to attend “8K Open House 2015” and go to TEPIA. In this event, There are many innovative technologies. If you ask me what innovation was inspired me, the answer is “Everything”. So I will bring my new knowledge to use for my work. In addition, the most impressive thing in this week is relaxing in the Shibuya with Shirai sensei and Om. We have time to play a game together, take selfies, talk and exchange our ideas. It makes us know each other better. It’s a wonderful week.
